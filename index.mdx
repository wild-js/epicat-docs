Epicat is a no-code/low-code AI agent platform that empowers teams to build, deploy, and orchestrate intelligent agent teams without technical expertise. With visual drag-and-drop tools, you can create sophisticated multi-agent workflows, connect with external APIs and services, and automate complex business processes. Whether you're building customer support assistants, data processing pipelines, or multi-step automation workflows, Epicat makes advanced AI capabilities accessible to everyone.
