Welcome to Epicat! This guide will walk you through the essential steps to get your account set up and create your first project.

## 1. Account Setup

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="user-plus" title="Create Your Account">
    Visit [epicat.com](https://epicat.com) and sign up for your free account. You'll need to verify your email address to get started.
    
    **What you'll need:**
    - Valid email address
    - Password (8+ characters recommended)
    - Organization name (can be changed later)
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="building" title="Set Up Your Organization">
    After account creation, you'll be prompted to create your first organization. This is where your team will collaborate on projects.
    
    **Organization settings:**
    - Choose a unique organization name
    - Set your organization's display name
    - Configure billing settings (if using paid features)
    - Set up organization-wide security policies
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## 2. Team Setup

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="users" title="Invite Team Members">
    Collaborate with your team by inviting members to your organization.
    
    **Steps:**
    1. Go to **Settings** > **Team Members**
    2. Click **Invite Member**
    3. Enter email addresses and select roles
    4. Send invitations
    
    **Available roles:**
    - **Admin**: Full access to organization settings and projects
    - **Developer**: Can create and manage projects
    - **Viewer**: Read-only access to projects
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="shield-check" title="Configure Permissions">
    Set up role-based access control for your team.
    
    **Permission levels:**
    - Project creation and deletion
    - Secrets management access
    - Deployment permissions
    - Billing and organization settings
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## 3. Create Your First Project

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="plus-circle" title="Project Creation">
    Projects are the core workspace where you'll build your AI agents and integrations.
    
    **To create a project:**
    1. Click **New Project** from your dashboard
    2. Choose a project name and description
    3. Select your preferred AI model provider
    4. Configure initial settings
    
    <Note>
      Your first project comes with free credits to help you get started!
    </Note>
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="robot" title="Set Up Your First Agent">
    Agents are AI-powered workers that can automate tasks for your team.
    
    **Quick agent setup:**
    1. Navigate to **Agents** in your project
    2. Click **Create Agent**
    3. Choose from templates or start from scratch
    4. Define your agent's role and capabilities
    5. Test your agent with sample inputs
    
    Popular agent templates:
    - Customer support assistant
    - Data processing agent
    - Content generation agent
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## 4. Essential Configuration

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="key" title="Set Up API Keys">
    Configure API keys for your AI model providers and integrations.
    
    **Supported providers:**
    - OpenAI (GPT-4, GPT-3.5)
    - Anthropic (Claude)
    - Google (Gemini)
    - OpenRouter (Multiple models)
    
    Go to **Settings** > **API Keys** to add your credentials securely.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="plug" title="Connect Integrations">
    Extend your project's capabilities with built-in integrations.
    
    **Popular integrations:**
    - Slack for team notifications
    - GitHub for code repositories
    - Webhooks for custom services
    - Database connections
    
    Visit the **Integrations** tab to explore available options.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## 5. Account Management

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="settings" title="Update Account Settings">
    Manage your personal account settings and preferences.
    
    **Available settings:**
    - Profile information
    - Email preferences
    - Security settings (2FA recommended)
    - API access tokens
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="trash" title="Delete Account">
    Need to delete your account? We're sorry to see you go.
    
    **Before deleting:**
    - Export any important project data
    - Transfer organization ownership (if applicable)
    - Cancel any active subscriptions
    
    **To delete your account:**
    1. Go to **Settings** > **Account**
    2. Scroll to **Danger Zone**
    3. Click **Delete Account**
    4. Follow the confirmation steps
    
    <Warning>
      Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your projects and data will be permanently removed.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
